The Method

Concept to production
in days, not months.

01
Domain-first, not technology-first
Every product starts with a real operational problem. The technology is chosen to solve it — not to demonstrate capability. This is why the tools work in the field, not just in demos. The user is always a practitioner, not a prototype tester.
02
Standalone by default
Every product is a self-contained deployable unit. No dependencies on external platforms, no vendor lock-in, no prerequisite infrastructure. Deploy to Railway, serve from GitHub Pages, or run locally. The IP travels.
03
The jigsaw doctrine
Every product is standalone — and every product connects. DIG feeds into LitmusLaw. LitmusLaw feeds into The Bench. The Bench feeds into ACMS. KTC feeds into Change2Crypto. The portfolio is not a collection of tools. It is an intelligence loop.
04
Security and privacy by design
Post-quantum aware from day one. Zero-knowledge architecture where possible — DIG stores no PII, WaterCycle stores nothing external, TheRealAgent keeps all data in the browser. Canary system, IP timestamping, and fraud detection woven through the stack.
